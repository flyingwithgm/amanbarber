About Us

Premium Barbering

At Amanfour Barbers Palace, we specialize in creating the perfect look for the modern gentleman. Our experienced barber combines traditional techniques with contemporary styles to deliver exceptional results.

Located in the heart of Tarkwa on Tamso New Road, we've been serving the community with premium grooming services for years. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us the go-to destination for men's grooming needs.

With state-of-the-art facilities and premium products, we ensure every visit is a premium experience. Our barber is trained in the latest techniques and trends to keep you looking your best.
